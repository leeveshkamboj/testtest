INNOVATE-A-THON
presented by UIET

"INNOVATE-A-THON” focuses on campus-oriented problems where young pioneers collaborate to solve problem statements and earn rewards. This is a 9-hour-long event to address some unease faced by our departments. It is a way to bring students together to provide innovative solutions to problems and can also contribute to open innovation. This includes problem statements as well as an open innovation category. Participants will have the opportunity to develop solutions for real-world issues. We welcome all aspiring innovators to join this exciting event and bring their ideas to life.

Categories

Software

Attendance Tracking System

Develop a system for easy and accurate recording of attendance during one-time events without using hardware or biometric data, preventing fraudulent practices such as proxy attendance. It should be secure, reliable, and easy to use, and provide real-time attendance tracking and reporting for event organizers. This would streamline attendance tracking, prevent fraud, provide tamper-proof records, and save time for organizers.

Automated Certificate Management System

The manual process of generating and distributing certificates is time-consuming, labor-intensive, and error-prone. Therefore, we require a solution that can automate the certificate management system for events. The solution should be able to generate unique certificates for each participant with a unique ID and deliver them automatically via email. Furthermore, the solution should include a verification system to ensure the authenticity of certificates.

AI-Based Employment Estimation in Technical Education

There is a need to develop an application software that uses data correlation and AI to estimate employment potential for courses in engineering, management, and pharmacy. This will help in granting approval for new technical institutions and courses, as well as forecasting current trends in admissions and job sectors. The software will ensure that courses and syllabus are adjusted to keep students skilled and employed in a rapidly changing world.

FAQ's

Can anyone participate in this hackathon?
+ -

Students enrolled in any UG courses in Kurukshetra University can participate in “INNOVATE-A-THON”.

Is there any prerequisites for participating?
+ -

All the members of the team must be from the same college.

Am I required to pay anything?
+ -

No, it's totally free. We only require your knowledge and enthusiasm.

Is it a team competition, can't I participate alone?
+ -

It is mandatory for each team to have 4 members. But in open innovation, Students can opt for individual or group (not exceeding 4 members in a team)

What's the prize if you end up winning? Give me some Incentive.
+ -

There is a butt load of a cash prize and swags up for grabs. Further details will be released soon.

How many teams will be shortlisted per problem statement?
+ -

Four teams will be shortlisted for each problem statement.

Can I change the team after registration?
+ -

You will not be allowed to make any changes to your team structure once registered.

How do I know if my idea has been selected or shortlisted?
+ -

Shortlisted teams will be notified about any important updates through email or WhatsApp.
